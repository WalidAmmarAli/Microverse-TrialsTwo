Here is briography victor jara life

  • Víctor Jara was born on September 28, 1932 in San Ignacio, into a family of peasants.
  • In 1953, at the age of twenty-one, he was part of the Choir of the University of Chile
  • During 1957, he joined the "Cuncumén" Folk Songs and Dances group, created as a result of some seasonal courses taught by Margot Loyola. He also had his first contacts with Violeta Parra, who encouraged him to continue singing.
  • He worked as a professor of acting between 1964 and 1967 at the Theater School of the University of Chile.
  • On September 11, 1973, Jara left for the State Technical University, his workplace, where he would sing at the opening of an exhibition, from which President Allende would address the country. The military surround the university campus, entering it the next day, taking all the teachers and students who were inside it into custody.
